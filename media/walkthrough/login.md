# 🔐 Connect Your Newton School Account

Once the extension is installed, the Newton School MCP server will automatically register with VS Code.

**How login works:**
1. Open **Copilot Chat** and ask any Newton-related question
2. The MCP server will start and open your **browser** for authentication
3. Log in with your Newton School credentials
4. You're all set! 🎉

The login is a one-time process. Your credentials are stored securely on your machine.
