# 📊 Your Newton School Dashboard

The sidebar dashboard gives you quick access to:

- **🔥 QOTD Streak** — Your Question of the Day streak
- **📚 Schedule** — Next upcoming class or event
- **📊 Progress** — Quick-action buttons for common queries

### Quick Actions
Click any button in the dashboard to instantly open Copilot Chat with the right question:

| Button | What it asks |
|--------|-------------|
| 📊 My Progress | "What's my course overview?" |
| 📝 Due Assignments | "What assignments are due this week?" |
| 🔥 Today's QOTD | "Show me today's Question of the Day" |
| 💻 Practice Problems | "Give me 5 medium difficulty DSA problems" |

Find the Newton School icon in your **Activity Bar** (left sidebar) to access the dashboard anytime!
